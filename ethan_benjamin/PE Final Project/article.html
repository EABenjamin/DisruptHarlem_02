Made for Otaku's, by Otaku's


Pariah Enterprises is a productions studio that focuses on communities dedicated to everything relating to Anime, Manga, Video Games and everything in between. Originally conceived in 2008 (As Pariah Industries), and officially founded in 2016, Pariah Enterprises focuses on crowd-sourced projects that is unique and affordable. It doesn't matter if the those in the community are just the average anime watcher, everyday manga and video game enthusiast, or one who considers themselves an "Otaku", Pariah Enterprises is here to please the masses by creating content for the community, by members of the community.

Goals:

1. Create quality content for communities dedicated to Manga, Anime and Video Games.

2. Promote a more mature atmosphere within the existing community.

3. Provide affordable content to the masses that meet and exceed community expectations.
